## Problem Identification

### Primary Problem
**Lack of accessible, accurate waste management guidance** leads to environmental pollution, inefficient resource utilization, and missed recycling opportunities.

### Problem Segmentation

#### Segment 1: Individual Households
- **Pain Points:** Confusion about waste sorting, lack of local disposal information, time-consuming research
- **Frequency:** Daily waste generation
- **Impact:** Environmental guilt, missed recycling, potential fines
- **Current Solutions:** Google search, municipal websites (often outdated), word of mouth

#### Segment 2: Small Businesses
- **Pain Points:** Regulatory compliance complexity, disposal costs, staff training needs
- **Frequency:** Daily commercial waste generation
- **Impact:** Financial penalties, operational inefficiency, reputation damage
- **Current Solutions:** Professional waste management services, expensive consultants

#### Segment 3: Municipal Workers
- **Pain Points:** Public education challenges, contamination issues, inefficient collection routes
- **Frequency:** Ongoing operations
- **Impact:** Increased operational costs, community dissatisfaction
- **Current Solutions:** Public campaigns, manual inspections

## Solution Validation
